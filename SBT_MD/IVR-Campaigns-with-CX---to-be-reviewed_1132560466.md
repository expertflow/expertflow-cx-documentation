# CX Knowledgebase : IVR Campaigns with CX - to be reviewed

IVR campaigns, also called IVR blasting or voice broadcasting, are an automated method for outbound communication. This technology enables businesses to simultaneously deliver pre-recorded voice messages to a large number of recipients.

Instead of an agent individually dialing each number, an automated system dials thousands of phone numbers at once and plays a consistent, pre-recorded message when the call is answered.

With Expertflow CX, you can create such IVR campaigns to deliver a pre-recorded message to your customers. 

## **Why Expertflow CX?**

  * Outbound Port monitoring

  * Multichannel outbound campaigns (IVR, SMS, WhatsApp, Email)

  * Retry mechanism

  * TCPA and Do not call lists
