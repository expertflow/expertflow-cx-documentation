# CX Knowledgebase : Security and Privacy Compliances

Expertflow CX adheres to globally recognized security and privacy regulations such as HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act), PCI-DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard), and GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation). We comply with these frameworks to ensure that our customers personal, financial, and healthcare data is well-protected. 

We are continuously working towards enhancing our security and privacy compliance to meet evolving industry standards. The following documents outline our current compliance status and our roadmap for full implementation:

  * [PCI DSS Compliance](PCI-DSS-Compliance_608927784.html)
