# Team Announcements

Supervisors can create announcements to notify their team agents of important information. Each announcement is displayed in the agent's notification bell and persists until its expiry time, or until the supervisor deletes it.

Agents receive announcements on login. An agent who is logged out when an announcement is created will receive it upon their next login — provided the announcement has not yet expired.

## Announcement Statuses

| Status | Description |
|---|---|
| **Scheduled** | Planned to be sent at a future date/time. Not yet visible to agents. |
| **Active** | Currently visible in agents' bell notification dropdown. |
| **Expired** | Past the expiry time; no longer visible to agents. |

---

## Creating an Announcement

1. Click the **Team Announcement** tab in the left menu bar.
2. Click **+ New Announcement**.
3. Fill in the announcement details:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Announcement Text** | The message content. Maximum 500 characters. |
| **Select Team(s)** | Choose one, multiple, or all of your teams. Only teams the supervisor manages appear in the dropdown. |
| **Schedule Date/Time** | When the announcement is triggered and sent to agents. Defaults to the current date/time. Use a future time for a scheduled announcement. |
| **Expiry Date/Time** | When the announcement disappears automatically. Defaults to 10 minutes from now. Maximum: 5 days from creation. |

4. Click **Save**.

---

## Editing an Announcement

- Only **Scheduled** announcements can be edited.
- **Active** and **Expired** announcements cannot be edited.

To edit:
1. Hover over the announcement in the Team Announcement list.
2. Click the **Edit** icon.
3. Update the fields and save.

---

## Deleting an Announcement

Announcements in any status (Scheduled, Active, or Expired) can be deleted.

1. Hover over the announcement in the list.
2. Click the **Delete** icon.

> Deleting an **Active** announcement removes it from the list and immediately disappears it from all agents' notification views.

---

## How Agents Receive Announcements

- Agents see announcements in their **bell notification dropdown** while logged in.
- An agent who is **logged out** when the announcement is created receives it upon their next login.
- Once an announcement expires, it is no longer visible — even if the agent has not read it.
- The supervisor who created an announcement does not receive it themselves.
