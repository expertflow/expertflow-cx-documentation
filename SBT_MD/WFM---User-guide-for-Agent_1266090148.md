# CX Knowledgebase : WFM - User guide for Agent

As an Agent, you can manage your schedule, requests, and daily activities in Workforce Management. This guide covers how to:

  * **View your personal schedule** in day, week, or month format to track assigned shifts, activities, and leave.

  * **Apply for leave** (full-day or partial-day) by selecting leave type, dates, and adding notes for supervisor approval.

  * **Check team schedules** to compare coverage and view colleagues’ assignments alongside your own.

  * **Submit and track requests** for leave, shift swaps, or trade posts, with real-time status updates.

  * **Use the swap board** to explore available shifts and participate in trades that comply with your skills and contracts.




## **My Schedule**

**Navigation**

Calendar → My Schedule

**Apply for Leave**

  1. Click **Apply for Leave** (top-right).

  2. Select **Leave Type** from the dropdown.

  3. Choose **Start Date / End Date**.

  4. (Optional) Enter **Start & End Time** for partial-day leave.

  5. Add a **Comment** if required.

  6. Click **Apply**.




Approved leave blocks will automatically prevent scheduling conflicts.

## **Swap Table**

**Navigation**

Calendar → Swap Table

  * See all publicly posted **swap requests** in a timeline view.

  * Each row shows the requester’s name, date, and time span of the shift offered.

  * Use this board to identify available trades you may be eligible to take.


![image-20250828-123518.png](attachments/1266090148/1267204147.png?width=1416)

## **Requests**

**Navigation**

Calendar → Requests

### **1\. Leave Requests**

  * View the status of submitted leave (Approved, Rejected, Pending).

  * Track type, duration, and requested time.


![image-20250828-123705.png](attachments/1266090148/1266385008.png?width=1416)

### **2\. Shift Swap Requests (Targeted)**

  * Review swaps where you are directly invited as the **Target**.

  * Check details like requester, subject, and notes.

  * Wait for system validation and supervisor approval.


![image-20250828-123737.png](attachments/1266090148/1266712637.png?width=1416)

### **3\. Shift Trade Posts (Open Market)**

  * View all posted shifts open for trade.

  * Check **validity dates** and **skills required**.

  * Apply to take shifts if you meet skill/contract criteria.
