# CX Knowledgebase : Dialing & Success Rate Summary - Campaign Wise

**Report Summary**|  Shows the dial or success rate of a campaign.  
---|---  
  
The metrics include: 

  * Total Dial Attempts.

  * Successful Attempts (Customer Connected).

  * Unsuccessful Attempts - Attempts that were not connected with the customer.

  * Total {failure-reason} Attempts - where **failure-reason** is any of the failure call results. 




## Report Columns

Following are the report columns.

**Field Name**| **Description**  
---|---  
Campaign Name| Name of the campaign  
Total uploaded contacts| The total number of customer contacts that were uploaded successfully  
Total dialed contacts| Total number of unique customer contacts that are dialed (regardless of the result)  
Total connected - IVR| Total number of customer contacts that were connected successfully  
Total connected - Agent| Total number of customer contacts that were connected successfully  
Total dialed attempts| Total number of dialed attempts on all customer contacts (could be multiple for each)  
Dial Rate %| (Total Dialed (unique contacts)/ Total Uploaded) * 100  
Success Rate %| (Total Connected/ Total Dialed (unique contacts)) * 100
