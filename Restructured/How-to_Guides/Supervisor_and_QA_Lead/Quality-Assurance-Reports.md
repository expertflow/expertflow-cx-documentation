# Quality Assurance Reports

As a Quality Manager (Quentin), you must track the efficiency of your evaluation program. The **Quality Assurance Reports** provide both real-time and historical views of your audit queue and scoring trends.

## 1. Evaluation Volume Report
This report tracks the status of all audits currently in the system. Use this to ensure your evaluators are meeting their weekly quotas.

### Data Views:
- **Planned:** Audits that have been scheduled but not yet started.
- **In-Progress:** Audits currently being worked on by an evaluator.
- **Completed:** Audits where the form has been submitted and the score is finalized.

## 2. Report Filters for Quentin
You can slice the data to find specific performance gaps:
- **Time Period:** View data by Day, Week, Month, or Year to see long-term quality trends.
- **Evaluator Productivity:** Filter by **Reviewer** to see how many audits each member of your team is completing.
- **Team Performance:** Filter by **Team** or **Agent** to see which groups are struggling with specific rubric criteria.
- **Form Impact:** Filter by **Questionnaire** to see if a particular evaluation form is consistently returning low scores (which may indicate the form is too difficult).

## 3. Data Visualization
The reports offer both **Tabular** (exact numbers) and **Graphical** (trend lines) views.
- Use the **Graphical View** to spot sudden dips in quality after a new product launch or script change.
- Use the **Tabular View** for payroll or performance review exports.
