# Phase 1 Summary: Documentation Inventory

Phase 1 of the ExpertFlow CX Documentation project focused on conducting an exhaustive inventory and classification of the existing knowledge base to prepare for architectural improvements and persona-based auditing.

## Key Objectives
- **Inventory Creation:** Mapping out the entire documentation hierarchy and breadcrumbs.
- **Classification:** Categorizing documents into functional buckets to determine their future in the new documentation structure.
- **Audit Preparation:** Identifying core content that needs to align with the 9 defined personas and Diátaxis framework.

## Inventory Statistics
A total of **662 pages** were inventoried and categorized as follows:

| Category | Count | Primary Action |
|----------|-------|----------------|
| **Core Documentation** | 556 | Audit for 9 Personas and Diátaxis compliance. |
| **Internal Drafts** | 7 | Review for potential improvement or deletion. |
| **Historical/Reports** | 39 | Archive or move to versioned documentation space. |
| **Legacy/Duplicates/Temp** | 61 | Label for removal or move to Review Tree. |

## Major Outputs
- **Exhaustive Inventory with Breadcrumbs:** A detailed mapping of 662 pages including hierarchy, slugs, and recommended actions.
- **Inventory Summary Report:** A high-level breakdown of document counts and categorization.
- **Classification System:** Established "Buckets" (A through D) for streamlined content management.
