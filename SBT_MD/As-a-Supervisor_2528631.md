# CX Knowledgebase : As a Supervisor

## Supervisor Capabilities  
  
This document describes the capabilities of Expertflow Contact Center Supervisors.

A CX Supervisor has default access to all [agents ](As-an-Agent_2529298.html)and additional features explained below. Also, see [Security and User Permissions](/wiki/pages/createpage.action?spaceKey=SBT&title=Security%20and%20User%20Permissions&linkCreation=true&fromPageId=2528631) to know the additional capabilities of a supervisor.

**Capabilities**| **Description**  
---|---  
[**Monitor Dashboards**](Realtime-Reports-and-Dashboards_2529305.html)|  The dashboards embedded in Agent Desk are the real-time dashboards for supervisors and agents to view the summary statistics of the contact center including queues, agent states, and the real-time conversations that are ongoing with agents and the bots.  
[**Control Agent team**](Add-Agents%2C-Supervisors%2C-and-Teams_242909198.html)|   
[**Silent Monitoring**](Silent-Monitoring_2529320.html)|  Enables a supervisor to monitor an ongoing conversation silently between the agent and the customer.  
[**Barge-in**](Barge-in_2529322.html)|  Enables a supervisor to monitor and barge in the conversation if required.  
[**Team Announcements**](Team-Announcements_2523883.html)|  Enables a supervisor to create, edit, and delete announcements for one's team agents.  
[**Force Logout Agent**](Force-Logout-Agent_1181941884.html)|  Enables a contact center supervisor to forcibly logout any agent, regardless of the agent’s current state, be it **Ready** , **Not Ready** , **in a conversation** , **Consult** , **Conference** , or **Handling Multiple Conversations**.  
This is available for supervisors on the [**Available** **Agents Detail Dashboard**](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SBT/pages/2529305/Realtime+Reports+and+Dashboards#Active-Agents-Detail)**.**  
[**Customer Lists**](Customer-List_441450562.html)|  Allows a supervisor to view Customer Lists to show the customer records.  
[**Customer Schema**](Customer-Schema_2525665.html)|  Allows the business administrator or anybody who has permission to customize the Customer fields.  
[**Customer Labels**](Customer-Labels_2529344.html)|  Allows the business to categorize customers based on certain characteristics such as customer segment, language, and type (gold, platinum, standard).   
[**Subscribed List**](Subscribed-Lists_442204167.html)|  Enables a supervisor to know about subscribed lists and how to join PULL based request from a List.  
[**End PULL-based Requests**](End-PULL-based-Requests_2528595.html)|  Enables a supervisor to end PULL-based requests.
