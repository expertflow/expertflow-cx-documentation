# CX Knowledgebase : Pre-Conversation Forms

[Expertflow Conversation Studio](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/x/1RiAB) can display a web form via WWW before the customer initiates a voice, video, or chat [session](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/x/DoCOBQ). The information collected can be used to make [routing decisions](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/x/F4sm) or display to the agent when the voice, video or chat lands. It is also attached as the [Conversation Data](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/x/AIwm) to the customer profile.
