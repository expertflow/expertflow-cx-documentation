# CX Knowledgebase : WebRTC Summary Report

**Report Summary**|  Shows the summary of the webrtc generated links.  
---|---  
  
## Report Columns

The following are the report columns

**Fields**| **Description**  
---|---  
DATE| Shows the date.  
Total Generated Links| Show the total link generated.  
Total Successful | Show the total successfully connected links.  
Total Failure| Shows the total of links that are not connected successfully.
