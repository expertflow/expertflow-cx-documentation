# Google Play Store Agent Guide

With Google Play Store integration, you can monitor and respond to user reviews on your Play Store applications directly from the **Expertflow CX Conversation View**.

## 1. Agent Capabilities

- **View Reviews and Star Ratings:** You can see the review text and star rating (1–5 stars) along with the app name and logo.
- **Send Public Replies:** You can send a public reply to a review from the Conversation View. Your reply will appear on the app’s Play Store listing under the customer’s review.
- **Threaded History:** View the complete history for a given review, including original text, historical ratings, and previous replies.

## 2. How to Reply to a Review

Follow these steps to respond to a customer review:

1.  **Locate the Review:** In the **Conversation View**, find the incoming review message.
2.  **Initiate Reply:** Hover over the message and click on the **reply** icon.
3.  **Confirm Channel:** Ensure that the selected channel is **Google Play Store**.
4.  **Compose Message:** Click in the Message Composer at the bottom of the screen and type your reply.
5.  **Send:** Click **Send**.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> All replies are subject to Google Play Store policies and may be moderated by Google.

## 3. Viewing the Review Context (View Full Post)

To see the full context of a review interaction:

-   Agents can view the **App name** and **Original review text**.
-   The **View Full Post** panel shows the top 4 reviews with the reviewer name, star ratings, review time, and threaded replies.
-   The thread appears within the Conversation View, so you do not need to leave the Agent Desk.

## 4. Reporting

The **Channel Session Detail** report includes all Google Play Store review conversations. This allows supervisors to track engagement metrics and response times for this channel.
