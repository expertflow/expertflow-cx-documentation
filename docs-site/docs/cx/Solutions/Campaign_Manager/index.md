## The Business Problem

Contact centers face a constant tension between reactive inbound support and proactive outreach. When agents must manually dial hundreds of customers for callbacks, payment reminders, or appointment confirmations, the process is slow, inconsistent, and expensive. Missed callback promises erode trust, and manual outreach at scale is simply not viable. ExpertFlow CX Campaign Manager solves this by automating proactive outreach across voice and digital channels — so your team reaches more customers with less effort and zero manual dialing.

## What You Can Build

Campaign Manager is ExpertFlow CX's outbound engagement solution. It lets you design and launch automated contact flows that reach customers via voice calls, IVR broadcasts, SMS, or WhatsApp — all without requiring a human agent for every interaction. Whether you need to honour callback promises, run promotional blasts, or send transactional alerts, Campaign Manager provides a single platform with multiple delivery variants to match the use case.

### Callbacks

When a customer requests a callback instead of waiting on hold, Campaign Manager automatically schedules and dials them back when an agent becomes available. Customers get served at a convenient time; agents handle a steady, predictable queue instead of scrambling to honour promises manually.

### IVR Blasters

Deliver pre-recorded voice messages or automated IVR prompts to thousands of contacts simultaneously — ideal for payment reminders, service alerts, or appointment confirmations. No agents are required; the system handles dialling, retries, and reporting end-to-end.

### SMS and WhatsApp Campaigns

Send targeted, personalised text or WhatsApp messages at scale using a "fire and forget" flow. Use it for promotional offers, one-time passwords, delivery notifications, or any outreach where a written message is more effective than a call.

## How It Works

1. **Define your audience.** Upload a contact list via CSV or apply dynamic filters to segment your customer database — for example, all accounts overdue by more than 30 days.
2. **Design the flow in Conversation Studio.** Use the visual Flow Builder to set the channel, dialling strategy, time windows, and retry logic. No code required.
3. **Launch — the platform takes it from there.** Campaign Manager handles delivery, retries on busy or no-answer, and records every attempt. Track results in real time from the Campaign Performance Reports dashboard.

## Supported Channels

| Channel | Supported | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Voice (IVR) | Yes | Supports pre-recorded messages and automated prompts; retries on busy or no-answer |
| Voice (Callback) | Yes | Schedules and dials customers back when an agent becomes available |
| SMS | Yes | One-way outbound delivery; no agent required; ideal for alerts, OTPs, and reminders |
| WhatsApp | Yes | One-way outbound delivery; no agent required; supports personalised messaging at scale |
| Email | — | Not currently supported |

## Key Outcomes

- Reduce inbound call volume by honouring callback promises automatically, without agent intervention.
- Reach large customer segments in minutes through IVR blasts or digital messaging campaigns.
- Improve agent utilisation by eliminating manual dialling and letting the platform manage pacing and retries.
