ExpertFlow CX supports two distinct survey approaches. Both share the same underlying form builder and Conversation Studio flows, but they differ in who initiates the survey and when.

## Two Survey Solutions

| | [Survey Campaigns](Survey-Campaigns.md) | [Post-Collaboration Surveys](Post-Collaboration-Surveys.md) |
|---|---|---|
| **Initiated by** | Business (outbound) | Platform (triggered by conversation end) |
| **When** | Any time — scheduled or event-driven | Immediately after a call, chat, or email |
| **Channels** | SMS, WhatsApp, Voice | All channels the conversation used |
| **Typical use** | NPS at scale, market research, follow-up campaigns | CSAT, NPS post-support, post-sales feedback |

---

## Shared Building Blocks

Both solutions are built on the same components:

- **Form Builder** — design survey questions (NPS, CSAT, MCQ, free text)
- **Conversation Studio** — control the flow, branching logic, and triggers
- **Unified Interaction History** — all responses stored as CX Activities for reporting
