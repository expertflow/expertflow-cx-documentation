ExpertFlow CX is a platform of components — Conversation Studio, Forms, Channels, Routing, Quality Management, and Workforce Management — that combine into complete business solutions. This section describes those solutions from the perspective of the outcomes they deliver, not the features they use.

---

## Solutions by Business Goal

| Business Goal | Solution | Supported Channels |
|---|---|---|
| Reach customers proactively — without an agent | [Campaign Manager](Campaign_Manager/index.md) | Voice (IVR blaster, callback), SMS, WhatsApp |
| Collect structured customer feedback at scale | [Survey Campaigns](Surveys/Survey-Campaigns.md) | SMS, WhatsApp, Voice |
| Measure satisfaction after every interaction | [Post-Collaboration Surveys](Surveys/Post-Collaboration-Surveys.md) | All channels (voice, chat, email, social) |
| Ensure the right agents are available at the right time | [Workforce Management](Workforce_Management/index.md) | — |
| Evaluate and improve interaction quality at scale | [Quality Management](Quality_Management/index.md) | All recorded channels |

## How Solutions Relate to Platform Capabilities

Each solution is built from underlying platform capabilities. Understanding this two-layer model helps during evaluation:

- **Solutions** answer *"what can I achieve?"* — this section.
- **Capabilities** answer *"what does the platform do?"* — see [Capabilities](../Capabilities/index.md).
- **How-to Guides** answer *"how do I configure it?"* — see [How-to Guides](../How-to_Guides/).
