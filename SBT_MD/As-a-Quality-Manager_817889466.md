# CX Knowledgebase : As a Quality Manager

As a **Quality Manager** , you can create or edit evaluation form, schedule evaluations, or individually assign them to evaluators. You can also monitor the overall progress of all the evaluation jobs.

To assign a user the Quality Manager role, see [this guide](/wiki/pages/createpage.action?spaceKey=SBT&title=IAM%20user%20Creation%20guide&linkCreation=true&fromPageId=817889466).

## Quality Management workflow for Quality Manager

  1. **Design Evaluation Forms**



  * Create [evaluation forms](Create-an-evaluation-form_819232775.html) with weighted sections and questions to ensure structured assessments.



  2. **Monitor Progress**



  * Track evaluation statuses (Pending, In Progress, Completed) on the [Review Screen.](Review-Screen_819593222.html)

  * Ensure timely completion of assigned evaluations.



  3. **Schedule Evaluations**



  * Assign bulk reviews to Evaluators using the [Review Scheduler](Review-Scheduler_817594518.html).

  * Apply filters such as agents, teams, Evaluators, and call specifics.

  * Configure one-time or recurring evaluations with set deadlines for completion.



  4. **Assign Evaluations**



  * Manually allocate individual evaluation tasks to Evaluators through the [Conversation List](Conversation-List_819101704.html).



  5. **Conversation View**



  * View and assess any conversation through [Conversation View](Conversation-View_822214946.html).

  * Option to review or assign conversations to an Evaluator.



  6. **Analyze Reports**



  * Access performance reports to evaluate trends and identify areas for improvement.




To understand how Quality Manager can perform their task, read [User Guide](User-Guide-for-Quality-Manager_818708526.html)
