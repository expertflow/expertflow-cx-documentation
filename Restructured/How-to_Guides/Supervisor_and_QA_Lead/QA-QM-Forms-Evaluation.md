# Quality Assurance and Quality Management Forms

Expertflow CX allows supervisors and AI bots to evaluate agent performance across all channels using customizable Quality Assurance (QA) forms.

## Scoring Rules
Through Conversation Studio, you can define specific rules for which conversations are marked for scoring:
- **By Experience**: Score one conversation per day for beginners, or once every three days for experts.
- **By Volume**: Score a fixed percentage of all interactions or all conversations within a specific queue.

## Evaluation Process
1. **Pull Mode**: Supervisors see a list of conversations marked "to-be-scored."
2. **Form Injection**: When a supervisor selects a conversation, the relevant QA form is automatically presented.
3. **Collaborative Scoring**: A conversation can be evaluated by multiple supervisors or a combination of a supervisor and an AI bot for cross-validation.

## QA Automation with Generative AI
AI can streamline the QA process by:
- Interpreting form questions and generating suggested responses based on chat/email transcripts or voice-to-text transcripts.
- Translating transcripts into the supervisor's primary language.
- Auto-filling compliance-related fields.

## Conversation Explorer
Use filters in the Conversation Explorer to find specific interactions for review based on agent tags, sentiment analysis, or previous low scores.
