# CX Knowledgebase : IVR Summary Report

**Report Summary**|  Summarizes the journey of IVR activities per day   
---|---  
  
## Report Columns

Following are the report columns

**Fields**| **Description**|   
---|---|---  
Date| Shows the date.|   
Language| Shows the chosen language of sumerized data.|   
Menu| Total number of times customers selected the Main Menu on the IVR.|   
Sub Menu| Total number of times customers selected the Sub Menu on the IVR.|   
Abandoned on IVR| Total number of Abandoned calls on IVR.|   
Transfer to Queue| Total number of calls transferred to Queue.|   
Total IVR Duration (HH:MM:SS)| Shows the sum of time the customer spent on IVR in (HH:MM:SS)|   
Average IVR Duration (S)| Shows the Average of time the customer spent on IVR in secounds.|   
IVR Utilization (%)| Show the IVR utilization in percentage.|   
Maximum IVR Duration (s)| Shows the Maximum time the customer spent on IVR in secounds.|   
  
## Report Filter

The following report filters are available for filtering the reporting data.

  * Date ~ choose the date for which you want to filter out the data.

  * Language ~ choose the language for which you want to filter out the data.
