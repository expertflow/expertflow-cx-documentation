# CX Knowledgebase : Real-time Contact Center Analytics

Expertflow provides integrated solutions for real-time contact center analytics, delivering live data streams and intelligence driving immediate operational decisions.

**Core Analytical Capabilities:**

  * **Live Operational Monitoring**

    * [**Queue & Resource Analytics**](Realtime-Reports-and-Dashboards_2529305.html#Queue-Stats): Real-time tracking of queue metrics (depth, wait time, SLA) and agent status across all channels.

  * **Live Interaction & Feedback Analysis**

    * [**Ongoing Conversation Detail**](Detailed-Dashboards_1322221656.html#Ongoing-Conversations-Detail): Real-time metadata and context for all ongoing customer conversations with agents and bots.

    * **Immediate Customer Feedback** : Integration of post-interaction CSAT scores and survey responses from [CX Surveys](https://docs.expertflow.com/cx/4.10/cx-surveys) for instant performance correlation.

  * **Proactive Intelligence & Automation**

    * **KPI Alerts** : A rule-based system for generating real-time notifications based on dynamic KPI thresholds.

    * **Live Conversation Analytics** : Analysis of interaction content as it occurs, enabling immediate quality management and agent guidance.




Expertflow Conversation Studio uses real-time system [events](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/x/DAD_MQ) to instantly deliver personalized IVR or bot flows. It then provides the full interaction context to the agent, ensuring a seamless and efficient handoff. 

Expertflow leverages advanced AI to generate powerful conversation analytics _while the customer interaction is still happening_ —whether the customer is speaking with a live agent or an automated bot. This shift from post-call review to live intelligence transforms how contact centers manage quality and customer experience across all touchpoints.

**AI-Powered Insights for Live Decision-Making**  
The system generates a live stream of actionable intelligence, including:

  * **Real-time Sentiment Detection:** Tracks the customer's emotional tone as it evolves during any interaction, flagging negative shifts instantly.

  * **Live AI-Generated Summaries:** Provides a running synopsis of the key discussion points—from both bot and agent dialogues—giving supervisors instant context.

  * **Automated Alerting:** Flags specific triggers like profanity, compliance risks, or dead-ends in a bot dialog based on configured rules.




**Proactive Intervention, Not Reactive Escalation**  
The core value lies in proactively identifying at-risk customers across the entire customer journey. By continuously analyzing the dialogue in both voice and digital channels, our AI detects rising frustration, confusion, or dissatisfaction in real-time. This triggers immediate, context-rich alerts to Supervisors, enabling them to:

  * **Intervene in Agent Conversations:** Step into a live call with a full understanding of the issue to prevent a negative outcome, without waiting for a formal agent escalation.

  * **Coach with Context:** Provide timely, specific guidance to agents via whisper messages, helping them steer the conversation positively without the customer being aware.




This eliminates the critical delay where a problem must first be recognized and manually escalated, saving valuable time and reducing customer effort.

**Seamlessly Escalate Bot Conversations:**

Identify when a bot is failing to meet a customer's needs and trigger a warm, context-rich transfer to a human agent, complete with the AI-generated summary of the issue so far.
