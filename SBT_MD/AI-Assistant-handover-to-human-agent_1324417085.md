# CX Knowledgebase : AI Assistant handover to human agent

During interactions with a virtual agent or IVR system, AI-driven conversation analytics generate summaries and detect customer sentiment in real time. If the system detects negative sentiment, it can proactively offer to transfer the customer to a human expert, preventing frustration and eliminating the need for the customer to ask.
