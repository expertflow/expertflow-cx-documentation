# CX Knowledgebase : Telegram Bot Token

## What is a Telegram Bot Token??  


A**bot token** is a unique identifier that can be used to send HTTPS requests to your bot. Or simply to control the behavior of your bot.

## How can I get my Telegram Bot Token ??

Follow this [guide](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/l/cp/V36ngh8m) to get your bot token.

Note

This token should be secretly stored and only share with people who need direct access to the bot. Anyone who has an access to your token can have full control of your bot.
