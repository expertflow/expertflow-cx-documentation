# CX Knowledgebase : Campaign Summary

**Report Summary**|  Shows the summary of campaign contact status grouped by call result.  
---|---  
  
## Report Columns

Following are the report columns

**Fields**| **Description**  
---|---  
Campaign Name| Name of the Campaign  
Total Loaded| Number of contacts uploaded into a campaign.  
Dialed (Unique)| Total number of dialed (unique) contacts.   
Dialed (Attempts)| Total number of dialed (attempted) contacts, _e.g., there are 3 contacts dialed, each at least 1 time, but the first contact was re-dialed a second time. So the total number in this column will be 4._  
Connected| The number of calls successfully connected.  
No Answer| The number of attempts where the dialer outcome was detected as "No Answer". However, the contact might still not be closed (rescheduled by the campaign strategy)  
Busy| The number of attempts where the dialer outcome was detected as "Busy". However, the contact might still not be closed (rescheduled by the campaign strategy)  
Not Connected| The number of contacts where the contact was dialed and was not connected for any reason. This pertains solely to voice calls and does not apply to SMS attempts  
Problem| All status codes in this group indicate instances while attempting to contact the customer.  
Dialer Abandoned| The number of attempts where the dialer outcome was detected as "Dialer Abandoned".  
Other| The number of attempts that resulted in reasons other than those mentioned above.
