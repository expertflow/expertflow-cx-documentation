# CX Knowledgebase : FAQs - WFM (To be updated by Nabeel after release)

![](images/icons/grey_arrow_down.png)How do I create a new contract?  
  
To create a new contract, navigate to **Configuration > Contracts**, then click the **Create Contract** button. Fill in the required details and click **Save**.

![](images/icons/grey_arrow_down.png)How can I edit an existing contract?

To edit an existing contract, go to **Configuration > Contracts**. Find the contract in the list, hover over it, and click the **Edit** icon. Make your changes and click **Save**.

![](images/icons/grey_arrow_down.png) What should I do if I encounter validation errors?

Check the form for any fields highlighted in red or accompanied by an error message. Ensure all required fields are filled out correctly and that all inputs adhere to the specified formats and constraints.

![](images/icons/grey_arrow_down.png)Can I delete a contract permanently?

Yes, contracts can be deleted permanently. Navigate to **Configuration > Contracts**, hover over the contract you want to delete, and click the **Delete** icon. Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears.

![](images/icons/grey_arrow_down.png)How do I search for a specific contract?

Use the search input field at the top of the **Manage Contracts** page. Type in the name or part of the name of the contract you are looking for to filter the results.

![](images/icons/grey_arrow_down.png) What are the acceptable formats for time inputs?

  * Weekly Rest Time: HHH:MM (max 167:59)

  * Nightly Rest Time: HH:MM (max 23:59)

  * Minimum/Maximum Time Per Week: HHH:MM (max 167:59)

  * Average Work Time Per Day: HH:MM (max 23:59)
