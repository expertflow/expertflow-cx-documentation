# CX Knowledgebase : Email

Email provides support to send and receive email to and from Expertflow Platform. Sending and receiving emails are the basic supported features. Alongside that, users can add attachments. Also, the reply feature and email threading are supported. 

In addition, forwarding emails, replying all are all available on our connector. Users can also use the Signature Feature to add their desired signature to the email. The details of all Channel capabilities are mentioned below.
