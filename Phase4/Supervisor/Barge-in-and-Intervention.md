# Barge-in and Intervention

As a Supervisor (Sam), there are moments when you must step in and take control of an interaction. The **Barge-in** feature allows you to join an active conversation as a primary participant.

## 1. How to Barge-in
Before you can intervene, you must first be silently monitoring a conversation.
1.  **Monitor First:** Find an active conversation in the **Ongoing Conversations Dashboard** and select **Silent Monitor**.
2.  **Barge-in:** Once you are monitoring, click the **Barge-in** button on your conversation toolbar.

## 2. Supervisor Experience: Voice vs. Chat
Your role and interface change depending on the channel you are barging into.

### For Voice Calls:
- **Primary Participant:** You join the call as an active party. Both the customer and the agent will hear you. 
- **The Conference:** The call becomes a three-way (or more) conference.
- **Presence:** Your state will automatically be set to **Not Ready** for the duration of the call.

### For Digital Chats:
- **Customer Tab:** You can send messages directly to the customer.
- **Whisper Tab:** You can send "Whisper" messages only to the agent.
- **Control:** You can also **Transfer** the conversation or **Leave** the chat once the issue is resolved.

## 3. Important Limitations
- **Sequence:** You cannot barge-in directly—you must always start with **Silent Monitoring**. 
- **Capacity:** A conversation can have a maximum of **four participants** in total.
- **Outbound Calls:** Barge-in is not supported for outbound or transferred voice calls.
- **Re-login:** If your permissions are updated in Keycloak, you must re-log into the Agent Desk to use the Barge-in feature.
