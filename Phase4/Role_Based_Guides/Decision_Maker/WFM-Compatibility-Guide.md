# WFM Compatibility Guide

Expertflow Workforce Management (WFM) is designed to integrate with enterprise-grade contact center environments.

## Current Compatibility
- **Cisco Unified Contact Center Enterprise (UCCE)**: Fully compatible. Enables direct access to contact data for scheduling and forecasting.

## Future Compatibility (Roadmap)
- **Cisco Unified Contact Center Express (UCCX)**: Integration planned.
- **Expertflow Contact Center (EFCX)**: Native integration planned for upcoming releases.

## Flexible Data Import
For systems not directly integrated, EF-WFM supports **Excel File Uploads**. This ensures that you can migrate data and generate schedules regardless of your current telephony provider.
