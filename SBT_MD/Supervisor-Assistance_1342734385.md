# CX Knowledgebase : Supervisor Assistance

Supervisors have a suite of dashboards and tools to monitor [agent performance](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/x/LIA6Tw), ensure service quality, and manage contact center operations.  
  
**Supervisor Dashboards & Monitoring**  
Supervisor [dashboards](https://docs.expertflow.com/cx-knowledgebase/latest/monitor-dashboards) provide [real-time statistics](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/x/GZgm) on [teams](https://docs.expertflow.com/cx-knowledgebase/latest/agent-team), [queues](Realtime-Reports-and-Dashboards_2529305.html#Queue-Stats), and [conversations](https://docs.expertflow.com/cx-knowledgebase/latest/detailed-dashboards#DetailedDashboards-OngoingConversationsDetail). [Supervisors](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/x/d5Um) use these dashboards to monitor ongoing customer interactions. If a negative customer sentiment is detected, the supervisor can review the conversation summary for quick context. Based on this assessment, they can:

  * Start a silent monitoring session.

  * [Whisper](Barge-in_2529322.html#Chat) a suggestion to the agent.

  * [Barge in](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/x/Kpgm) to directly handle the customer.




Agents can also proactively request help using the[ Hand-Raise](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/x/nQD4Hg) option, which sends a notification to the supervisor and allows them to join the chat session to guide the agent.

Furthermore, supervisors can configure alerts for SLA violations, enabling timely intervention and agent guidance.

**AI-Powered Assistance**  
Under [Supervisor AI Assistance](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/x/HICTTg), supervisors can access [summaries](Conversation-Analytics_1324089511.html#Comprehensive-Summaries) of conversations and [transcripts](Conversation-Analytics_1324089511.html#Live-Transcripts) of voice calls, complete with [sentiment analysis](Conversation-Analytics_1324089511.html#Sentiment-%26-Emotion-Analysis), to quickly understand the context of interactions.

**Supervisor Configuration Tools**  
Supervisors can use [Business Calendars](https://docs.expertflow.com/st/13.5/announcements-business-calendars#id-\(13.5\)Announcements&BusinessCalendars-BusinessCalendars) to define schedules for weekdays, weekends, office hours, and holidays. They can also configure the IVR by selecting one or a group of prompts to be played based on different criteria, such as:

  * The caller's region.

  * The particular Service Dialed Number (DN).

  * A specific time period.
