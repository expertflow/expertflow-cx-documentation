# Workforce Management (WFM) FAQs

## Contracts

<details>
<summary>How do I create a contract?</summary>

Navigate to `Configuration > Contracts` and click **Create Contract**.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What are the time formats?</summary>

Use `HHH:MM` for weekly totals (max 167:59) and `HH:MM` for daily work/rest.
</details>

## Shift Categories

<details>
<summary>What is a short name?</summary>

A 3-character code used for compact views in the scheduler grid.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I delete a category?</summary>

Yes, but ensure no active shifts are using it first, or reassign them to avoid errors.
</details>

## Troubleshooting

<details>
<summary>Why am I seeing validation errors?</summary>

Check for red-highlighted fields. Ensure time values do not exceed `23:59` for daily limits.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I search for a contract or category?</summary>

Use the search bar at the top of the **Manage** pages to find specific contracts or categories by name.
</details>
