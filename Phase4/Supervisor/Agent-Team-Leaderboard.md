# Agent & Team Leaderboard

The **Agent & Team Leaderboard** provides a ranked view of performance based on quality evaluation results. It is used to compare performance across teams and individual agents against organizational benchmarks.

## Key Metrics
- **Number of Reviews**: Total evaluations completed for the agent or team.
- **Average Scores**: The mean performance score based on questionnaire responses.
- **Score Variance**: Standard deviation of scores, helping to identify consistency or performance gaps.

## Purpose
- **Identify Top Performers**: Recognize agents with consistently high scores.
- **Target Coaching**: Identify low-performing agents or teams requiring additional training.
- **Benchmark Comparison**: Compare current performance against defined KPIs.

## Report Filters
- **Date/Time**: Filter by specific evaluation periods.
- **Team**: Multi-select filter for specific organizational units.
- **Agents**: Individual agent selection filter.
- **Questionnaire**: Filter by specific Evaluation Forms (Questionnaires) used.
