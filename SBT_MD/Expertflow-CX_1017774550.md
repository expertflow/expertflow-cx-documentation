# CX Knowledgebase : Expertflow CX

Expertflow CX is an AI‑native omnichannel inbound and outbound customer collaboration platform across voice, digital, and social channels. It unifies routing, quality management (QM), workforce management (WFM), AI, and analytics on a single, open architecture that can run standalone or as an overlay to existing Cisco contact centers.

This knowledge base provides product overviews, deployment options, integrations, and migration guidance from legacy digital-only solutions to the full Expertflow CX platform.
