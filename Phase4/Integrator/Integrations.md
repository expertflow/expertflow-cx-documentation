# Integrations Overview

ExpertFlow CX integrates with a wide range of third-party platforms — AI engines, contact centers, CRM systems, and workforce management solutions. It also provides SDKs for building custom channel connectors and agent/customer frontend applications.

---

## CRM Connectors

Connect CX to CRM platforms (Salesforce, HubSpot, SAP, and others) to surface customer data in the Agent Desk and sync conversation outcomes back to the CRM.

See [CRM Connectors](CRM-Connectors.md).

---

## AI Engine Integrations

Integrate LLM-based and NLU-based AI engines to power bots, sentiment analysis, suggestions, and automated conversation routing. Built-in connectors are available for Rasa and Dialogflow. Custom bot connectors can be developed using the Bot Connector Developer Guide.

See [Bot Connector Developer Guide](Bot-Connector-Developer-Guide.md) and [Add Bot Connector](Add-Bot-Connector.md).

---

## Contact Center Integrations

Integrate CX with Cisco UCCX and UCCE for voice channel routing, agent state synchronization, outbound dialing, and screen pop.

See [Cisco Contact Center Integration Reference](Cisco-Contact-Center-Integration-Reference.md) and [Cisco Voice Channel Configuration](Cisco-Voice-Channel-Configuration.md).
