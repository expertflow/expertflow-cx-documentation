# AI and Self-Service Capabilities

Expertflow CX unifies channels, routing, and AI to deliver autonomous customer self-service while empowering agents with intelligent assistance.

## Outcomes
- **Volume Deflection**: Handle routine queries via natural conversation on Voice, WhatsApp, and Web.
- **Reduced AHT**: Live transcripts and suggested answers speed up resolution.
- **BYO-AI**: Use your preferred AI engines (Google, OpenAI, Azure, Rasa) with no vendor lock-in.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Conversational Self-Service
- **Omnichannel**: Deploy bots across Web, WhatsApp, and Telephony (Conversational IVR).
- **Contextual Handover**: Seamlessly transition from bot to human agent with full history preserved.

### 2. Agent Assist
- **Transcription**: Real-time punctuation and transcription for voice calls.
- **Translation**: Bi-directional translation for multilingual support.
- **Suggestions**: One-click insert of knowledge snippets and drafting of replies.

### 3. Knowledge Integration (RAG)
- Ground AI answers in your own PDFs, websites, and CRMs to ensure compliant and accurate information.

## Strategic Roadmap
1. **Pilot Use Cases**: Start with high-volume intents like order status or password resets.
2. **Select Engines**: Choose ASR/TTS/LLM providers based on language and data residency needs.
3. **Measure**: Track containment rates and agent adoption to refine AI policies.
