# CX Knowledgebase : Supervisor Access to Unified Admin

Supervisors can login to Unified Admin to view and manage their team agents and their skill sets. 

As a supervisor, you can access and make changes to the following pages in Unified Admin:

  * Routing Engine => Agent Channel Categories 

  * Routing Engine => Agent Attributes 

  * Routing Engine => Queues 

  * Teams 




## Change Agent Channel Categories

To change the settings of the channel categories for the desired agents of your team. Expand the Routing Engine tab from the left and go to **Agent Channel Categories.**

Select the desired team from your supervised list of teams from the **Teams** dropdown. Search by agent’s username to look for the desired agent. 

![image-20250625-075235.png](attachments/1160151146/1160577031.png?width=1188)

## Change Agent Attributes

To change the agent attributes settings for the desired agents of your team. Expand the Routing Engine tab from the left and go to **Agent Attributes.**

Select the desired team from your supervised list of teams from the **Teams** dropdown. Search by agent’s username to look for the desired agent.

![image-20250625-080130.png](attachments/1160151146/1160184259.png?width=1191)

## Changing Queue Settings

As a supervisor, you can edit the queues assigned to your teams only and cannot edit others or, add new queues.

To change the queue settings of a desired queue, expand the Routing Engine tab from the left and go to **Queues.**

Select the desired team from your supervised list of teams from the **Teams** dropdown. Search by queue name to look for the desired queue.

![image-20250625-080559.png](attachments/1160151146/1159659992.png?width=1192)

## Manage Team Settings

As a supervisor, you can change your team definition, by adding, removing agents to/from the team. Also you can add new teams. The newly created teams will automatically be assigned to you. 

As a supervisor, you can manage settings of your team or create new teams. 

To change your team settings, go to **Teams** on the left menu.

You will see all the teams which are supervised by you on the **Teams** list. Click on **New Team** to add a new team under your supervision, or click any existing team to update the Team definition. 

![image-20250625-082923.png](attachments/1160151146/1159856670.png?width=1183)
