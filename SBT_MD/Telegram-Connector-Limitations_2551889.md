# CX Knowledgebase : Telegram Connector Limitations

Below is the list of limitations in our Telegram Connector.

  1. Telegram connector does not support delivery notifications.

  2. A Telegram customer can see typing indicator. However, the agent may not see the customer typing. The Telegram API does not provide this information. 

  3. When an image, video and audio is sent from telegram as a file, it is not properly shown on the agent desk.

  4. As an agent, you cannot use quoted reply when the customer is on Telegram.

  5. Emojis are not supported on the AgentDesk.

  6. Some [media file formats](Telegram-Connector-Media-Types-Support_67665941.html) are not supported on the AgentDesk (mp3, jpeg, rtf).

  7. Inability to seek or navigate audio received from Telegram chat on AgentDesk
