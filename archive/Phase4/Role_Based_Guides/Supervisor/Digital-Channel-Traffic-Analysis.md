# Digital Channel Traffic Analysis

The **Digital Channel Traffic Analysis** report provides a high-level summary of how your communication channels are being used. As a Supervisor (Sam), use this to identify which channels are most popular and which are experiencing the highest abandonment rates.

## 1. Traffic Metrics by Channel
This section summarizes all conversations opened for a particular channel during a specific timeframe.

- **Total Channel Sessions:** The cumulative count of sessions opened (e.g., total WhatsApp chats).
- **Average Session Duration:** How long a customer typically remains active on the channel.
- **Maximum Session Duration:** The single longest session recorded, useful for identifying outlier interactions.

## 2. Session Disposition Visualization (Graphs)
The report includes a **Doughnut Chart** that visually represents why sessions are closing.

### Disposition Slices:
- **% Customer:** Sessions closed by the user (healthy resolution).
- **% Agent:** Sessions closed manually by your team.
- **% Inactivity:** Sessions timed out due to customer silence (indicates potential engagement issues).
- **% Network:** Sessions lost due to technical connectivity errors.

## 3. Views & Filtering
You can toggle between different data perspectives:
- **Interval View:** See traffic in 15-minute windows to identify peak rush hours.
- **Weekly View:** Monitor long-term volume growth or seasonal trends.
- **Channel Type Filter:** Compare WhatsApp vs. Facebook vs. Webchat performance.
