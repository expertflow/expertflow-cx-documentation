# CX Knowledgebase : BotFather

**BotFather** is a bot on Telegram that creates and manages all the bots that you create via your account on Telegram.

You can reach him by searching [@BotFather](https://t.me/botfather) on Telegram and you should see this profile with a **verified tick** from telegram.

  


![](attachments/2526366/2551835.png?height=400)  


## What can we do with Bot Father ??

  1. Create our own telegram bots for our business or for personal use.  

  2. Edit and customize your existing bots. Eg description, Profile Picture, Picture description, About, Name, etc.. see [this](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/l/cp/JhyWo5b8).
  3. List all bots configured with your account
  4. Retrieve your bot’s token.
  5. Delete your bot
