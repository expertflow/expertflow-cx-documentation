# Digital Channel Management: Overview

Expertflow CX provides a unified framework for managing customer interactions across a wide spectrum of digital touchpoints. This index categorizes our supported digital channels, providing links to their functional specifications and configuration guides.

## 1. Messaging & Social Media

These channels allow for both synchronous and asynchronous engagement with customers on their preferred social platforms.

| Channel | Functional Overview | Key Capabilities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **WhatsApp** | [WhatsApp Channel Overview](./WhatsApp-Channel-Overview.md) | Support for Meta Cloud API and 360dialog, rich media, and templates. |
| **Facebook & Instagram** | [Facebook Channel Overview](./Facebook-Channel-Overview.md) | Direct messaging (DM) and social media comment-to-chat management. |
| **Twitter (X)** | [Twitter Channel Overview](./Twitter-Channel-Overview.md) | Manage direct messages and public mentions within the Agent Desk. |
| **LinkedIn** | [LinkedIn Channel Overview](./LinkedIn-Channel-Overview.md) | Integration for enterprise social engagement and customer support. |
| **Telegram** | [Telegram Channel Overview](./Telegram-Channel-Overview.md) | Full bot support and rich media handling via Telegram bots. |
| **YouTube** | [YouTube Connector Overview](./YouTube-Channel-Overview.md) | Manage video comments as chat sessions and reply from Agent Desk. |
| **Google Play Store** | [Google Play Store Channel Overview](./Google-Play-Store-Channel-Overview.md) | Monitor and reply to app reviews directly from the contact center. |

## 2. Core Digital Channels

The foundation of modern digital customer service, including web-based engagement and traditional electronic messaging.

- **[Web Chat (Customer Widget)](./Customer-Widget-Features-Capabilities.md):** Embeddable, customizable real-time engagement for your website or portal.
- **[Email Management](./Email-Channel-Overview.md):** Centralized handling of IMAP, SMTP, and Microsoft Exchange (O365) email accounts.
- **[SMS/MMS via SMPP](./SMPP-Channel-Overview.md):** High-volume text messaging support via standard SMPP protocols or [Twilio](../../Solution_Admin/Twilio-SMS-MMS-Configuration-Guide.md).

## 3. Voice & Real-time Media

While managed as a distinct real-time media category, Voice and Video are core components of the Expertflow CX omnichannel experience.

- **[Voice & Video Overview](../Voice_Real-time_Media/Voice-and-Video-Overview.md):** Functional specifications for inbound/outbound calling and video engagement.
- **[Web & App Calls Overview](../Voice_Real-time_Media/Web-App-Calls-Overview.md):** Using WebRTC for browser and mobile-based voice/video interaction.
- **[WebRTC for CX](../Voice_Real-time_Media/WebRTC-for-CX.md):** High-fidelity, browser-based voice and video communication.
- **[CX Dialer Reference](../Voice_Real-time_Media/CX-Dialer-Reference.md):** Capabilities for automated and manual outbound voice campaigns.

## 4. Custom Channel Development

For proprietary apps or emerging digital platforms not supported out-of-the-box, Expertflow CX offers a robust developer framework.

- **[Channel Connector SDK](../../Integrator/Channel-Connector-Developer-Guide.md):** Build your own media connector to bridge any external message source into the CX Routing Engine.
- **[Bot Connector SDK](../../Integrator/Bot-Connector-Developer-Guide.md):** Integrate custom NLU or AI-driven bots across any of the digital channels listed above.
