# As an Evaluator

As an Evaluator in ExpertFlow CX Quality Management, your role is to review conversations assigned to you by Quality Managers, assess agent performance using evaluation forms, and submit completed evaluations within the given deadline.

Evaluators can only access conversations explicitly assigned to them — they do not have broad access to the full conversation library.

## Quality Management Workflow for Evaluators

### 1. Access Assigned Evaluations

Open the **Review Screen** to see all evaluations assigned to you, categorized by status:

| Status | Description |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Assigned but not yet started. |
| **In Progress** | Opened but evaluation not yet submitted. |
| **Completed** | Evaluation submitted. |

### 2. Review Conversations

1. Click on an assigned evaluation to open the **Conversation View**.
2. Review the full interaction content:
   - **Chat**: Read the full message history between the customer and agent.
   - **Voice**: Listen to the call recording.
3. Note the context, agent behaviour, and any relevant details before scoring.

### 3. Complete the Evaluation Form

The assigned evaluation form is displayed alongside the conversation in the same interface.

1. Answer each weighted question in the evaluation form.
2. Provide additional written feedback where required.
3. All form fields marked as mandatory must be completed before submission.

### 4. Submit the Evaluation

1. Review your scores and feedback.
2. Click **Submit** to finalize the evaluation.
3. Ensure all assigned evaluations are submitted before their due date.

## What Evaluators Cannot Do

- View conversations that have not been explicitly assigned to them.
- Modify evaluation form templates (this is a Quality Manager responsibility).
- Assign evaluations to other evaluators.
