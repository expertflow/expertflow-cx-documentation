# CX Knowledgebase : Google Play Store

With Google Play Store integration, you can monitor and respond to user reviews on your Play Store applications.

## Agent Capabilities

  * **View Reviews and Star Ratings** : Agents can view the Review text and Star Rating (1–5 stars) along with the app name and app logo. 

  * **Send a public reply to a review** : Agents can send a public reply to a review directly from the Conversation View. The reply appears on the app’s Play Store listing under the customer’s review.




## Setup Steps

  1. Get a Google Service account [Onboarding Guide](Onboarding-Guide_1511653389.html)

  2. Configure Play Store as a Channel in Unified Admin [Configurational Guide](Configurational-Guide_1658126350.html)




## Agent How-to

### **To reply to a review**

  1. In Conversation View, hover over the message and click on the **reply** icon.

  2. Confirm that the selected channel is **Google Play Store**.

  3. Click in the Message Composer at the bottom of the screen.

  4. Type your reply message.

  5. Click **Send**.


![Reply_to_Review.gif](attachments/1647443969/1681227796.gif?width=800)

All replies are subject to Google Play Store policies and may be moderated by Google.

### View Full Post

Agents can view the complete history for a given review, including:

  * App name

  * Original review text

  * Top 4 reviews with the review name, their ratings in stars, review time, and any replies shown in a threaded view.


![Full.gif](attachments/1647443969/1716879435.gif?width=800)

The review thread appears within the Conversation View, so agents do not need to leave Agent Desk to see the full context.

The [Channel Session Detail](Channel-Session-Detail_2527421.html) report is included with this connector. It provides one row per channel session, allowing you to track each Google Play Store review conversation handled in Expertflow CX.
