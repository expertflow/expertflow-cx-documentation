# AI-Powered Quality Assurance

Traditional QA is limited by manual sampling, often covering less than 2% of interactions. The ExpertFlow AI module solves this by providing automated, objective evaluations for 100% of interactions.

## 1. Operational Modes
You can configure the system to balance AI automation with human expertise.

- **Full Automation:** AI evaluates and scores 100% of closed interactions. This provides immediate, exhaustive visibility into team performance.
- **Hybrid Mode:** Allows you to split the workload. For example, AI scores 80% of interactions, while the remaining 20% (high-value or complex cases) are assigned to **Human Evaluators (Eva).**

## 2. The Evaluation Engine
The AI engine processes conversation transcripts and metadata using Large Language Models (LLMs) to answer the specific criteria defined in your **Evaluation Forms.**
- **Objectivity:** Every interaction is scored against the same mathematical logic, removing human bias.
- **Scalability:** The system can process thousands of audits per hour without increasing headcount.
- **Language Support:** Currently supports English and Arabic (including various dialects).

## 3. Integration with Workflow
AI-generated scores flow directly into the standard **Quality Assurance Reports.**
- **Real-time Feedback:** Scores are available immediately after a conversation closes.
- **Coaching Integration:** Identify common failure points detected by the AI to drive targeted training sessions for **Agents (Amy).**
