# CX Knowledgebase : As a Conversation Designer

## Log in to Unified Admin 

As an administrator, you can log in to Unified Admin using your credentials.

![login.png](attachments/512098334/545062914.png?width=702)

  * Upon login, you will land on the dashboard, which will give you access to various solutions, including _Forms_ , _Surveys_ , _Campaigns_ , and more. Click on _Forms_.


![dashboard.png](attachments/512098334/544997379.png?width=763)

## Creating a form

To create a survey form, click on the **[+ New Form]** button. It will open up the form builder where you can add the different types of questions (including, but not limited to, NPS, MCQs, and 5-Star). For more details, see the [form creation guide](Form-Builder_866812373.html).

## Add a Survey

To add a new survey, 

  1. Get access to the control flow.

  2. In the control flow, you can run the survey (form) by inserting the [Message Survey Node](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/CXDOC/pages/1351025594/Survey+Message) at any stage of the conversation




**Deploying the Flow** :

Once all configurations are completed, click the **Deploy** button to activate the survey.

![Deploying the Survey_.png](attachments/512098334/568131589.png?width=704)

**Limitations in CX Surveys**

  1. By default, the System supports only one survey at a time. 

  2. In any case, if the server crashes, all the hash-map data will be lost and the form will be in accessible.
