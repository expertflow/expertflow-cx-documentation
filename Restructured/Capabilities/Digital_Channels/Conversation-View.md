# Conversation View

The Conversation View is a structured interface that gives Quality Managers and Evaluators access to full interaction content — messages, call recordings, and conversation data — alongside the evaluation form. It is channel-agnostic and supports all ExpertFlow CX channels; WebChat, CX Voice (Cisco UCCE), and Email channels have passed formal QA validation.

## Role-Based Access

### Quality Managers

Quality Managers have full control over conversation review and assignment:

- **Assign to Evaluator**: Assign a conversation to a specific evaluator. Select the evaluation form, set a due date, and configure reminder notifications.
- **Review Conversation**: Review and evaluate a conversation directly, without assigning it to an evaluator.
- **Filter by type**: Apply conversation type filters (chat, call, or all) to narrow the review scope.

### Evaluators

Evaluators have a streamlined view restricted to conversations assigned to them by a Quality Manager:

- Access and view assigned conversation details.
- Complete evaluations using the assigned evaluation form — displayed alongside the conversation content within the same interface.

## Conversation Content

The Conversation View pane displays all available content depending on the interaction type:

| Content Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Chat messages** | Full message history between customer and agent, in chronological order. |
| **Call recordings** | Audio and video recordings for voice and video interactions. |
| **Conversation data** | Structured data attached to the session (e.g., pre-conversation form submissions, customer profile fields). |

## Integration with Evaluation Forms

The evaluation form is embedded directly within the Conversation View — evaluators do not need to switch between screens to complete an assessment. Both the conversation content and the form are visible in a single interface.
