# CX Knowledgebase : Conversation Volume by Disposition

**Report Summary**|  Shows a bar chart display of the total number of conversations handled by Agent, Bot and Network  
---|---  
  
Each bar on the chart shows a count of conversations with the following dispositions:

  * Bot handled

  * Agent handled

  * Abandoned 




This helps the business to see the volume of conversations that were handled by a self-service bot, handled by agents, or abandoned by customers.
