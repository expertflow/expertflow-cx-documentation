# CX Knowledgebase : CX-Core platform

CX-Core is the platform that runs the core services including channel management, agent management and routing, rooms and activities, and customer interaction profiles. 

CX-Core (aka Expertflow CX) is the underlying platform of Expertflow CX that includes digital and social media channels, AgentDesk, customer-widget, ETL, Conversation Studio, Control Flow, Outbound flow nodes.
