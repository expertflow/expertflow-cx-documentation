# Evaluator Guide

As an Evaluator, your job is to review assigned conversations and submit scored evaluations before their deadlines. All your work lives in the **Reviews** tab.

---

## Step 1: Find Your Assigned Evaluations

Open the **Reviews** tab. Evaluations are grouped by status:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Assigned, not yet started |
| **In Progress** | Opened but not yet submitted |
| **Completed** | Submitted |

Sort by **Due Date** to prioritize work that is due soonest. Each row also shows which **Questionnaire (Rubric)** is assigned, so you know the scoring criteria before you start.

---

## Step 2: Open the Audit Workspace

Click the **Start Review** icon on any Pending evaluation. The system opens a unified workspace with:

- **Conversation transcript** or **media player** (voice recording / screen capture) on one side
- **Evaluation form** on the other side

This side-by-side layout means you can score as you review without switching windows.

---

## Step 3: Score the Interaction

Work through the evaluation form as you review the conversation:

1. Answer each weighted question based strictly on the rubric criteria.
2. If you deduct points, add a note explaining why — these notes are used for agent coaching.
3. Use non-scored text fields for qualitative feedback where required.
4. All mandatory fields must be filled before you can submit.

---

## Step 4: Submit

Click **Submit** when all fields are complete. The system calculates the final score automatically and moves the evaluation to your **Completed** list.

> You can only access conversations explicitly assigned to you. Designing evaluation forms and assigning evaluations are Quality Manager responsibilities.

---

## Staying on Top of Deadlines

Check the **Notifications bell** (top right) for:
- New assignment alerts when the Quality Manager assigns a batch to you
- Deadline reminders for evaluations approaching their due date
