# Finding Conversations for Audit

As a Quality Manager (Quentin), the **Conversation List** is your primary tool for data discovery. It allows you to filter through thousands of interactions to find the specific "moments of truth" that require auditing by your team of Evaluators (Eva).

## 1. Advanced Filtering Options
Use these filters to narrow down the interactions displayed in your workspace:

| Filter Category | Recommended Use Case |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Date & Time Range** | Isolate calls during a specific campaign or after a major system update. |
| **Agents & Teams** | Focus on a specific "low-performing" team or a new hire's onboarding progress. |
| **Wrap-up Codes** | Identify "Refund Requested" or "Technical Issue" calls for root-cause analysis. |
| **Interaction Direction** | Filter by Inbound (customer-led) or Outbound (proactive campaigns). |
| **Conversation Duration** | Find abnormally short calls (possible hang-ups) or very long calls (complex issues). |

---

## 2. Reviewing Metadata
The Conversation List table provides a high-level snapshot of interaction health before you even open the transcript:

- **Participants:** See both the Customer and the Agent(s) involved.
- **Reviews Status:** Quickly see if a conversation has already been audited and what the final score was.
- **Activities Count:** Identifies how many "back-and-forth" turns occurred in the chat.

---

## 3. Workflow Integration
From the Conversation List, you can perform the following actions:

1.  **Assign for Audit:** Right-click an interaction to move it into an Evaluator's "Reviews" queue.
2.  **View Full Transcript:** Click any entry to open the [Conversation View](../Functional_Areas/Digital_Channel_Management/Conversation-View.md).
3.  **Export for Training:** Select multiple interactions to export them as clinical training samples for the Agent Desk.
