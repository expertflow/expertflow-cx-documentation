# CX Knowledgebase : Queue Flushed Conversation Count

**Report Summary**|  Displays the count of conversations closed forcefully in each queue by the administrator or supervisor  
---|---  
  
By viewing this report, an administrator or supervisor can determine the number of conversations flushed out from this particular queue

This report shows the count of conversations in the form of a bar graph. The Queue name is shown on the X-axis, and the count of flushed conversations is shown on the Y-axis. 

![](attachments/2532043/2550483.gif?width=600)

## Report Filters

  * Date/Time - Choose the date to filter the data

  * Queue \- Choose the queue/queue(s) from the dropdown filter and **APPLY FILTERS**
