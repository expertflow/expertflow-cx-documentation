# CX Knowledgebase : How to customise your Telegram Bot

We can customize our bot's Name, about information, description, profile picture, description picture etc.  
  
For you to customize your bot. You will need to initiate a chat with [@BotFather](https://t.me/botfather). 

Don't know what is Bot Father?? [Learn more ](https://expertflow-docs.atlassian.net/l/cp/cYBJZV7E)

## Steps to customize your bot

  1. Search for [@BotFather](https://t.me/botfather) in telegram and start a new conversation.  
  


Note

It should be **verified with the tick icon** from the Official telegram.

Example screenshot:  
  
![](attachments/2526374/2551841.png?width=598)   
  


  2. Send the command _/mybots _it will list all your available bots created by your account. From the list select the bot that you want to edit.  


_/mybots_  
---  
  
  3. Once the bot is selected, you can edit accordingly.
