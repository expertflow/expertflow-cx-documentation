# Customer Journey Orchestration (CJO)

Customer Journey Orchestration (CJO) represents a shift from channel-centric operations to experience-centric design. It connects fragmented touchpoints into unified, context-aware experiences.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Unified Context Across All Channels
CJO maintains a complete timeline of interactions regardless of whether the customer uses web, phone, chat, or email. Agents see the "full story," picking up exactly where the last interaction ended.

### 2. Intelligent Resource Orchestration
The system automatically routes customers to the most appropriate resource:
- **AI Agents**: Handle routine questions and KB lookups.
- **Automated Workflows**: Verify identity and process simple transactions.
- **Human Agents**: Provide empathy and handle complex judgment-based issues.

### 3. Seamless Transitions
CJO ensures that context is carried through every handoff (e.g., from an AI Bot to a human agent), eliminating the need for customers to repeat information.

### 4. Proactive Engagement
Identify behavioral patterns to trigger proactive flows, such as outbound reminders or personalized offers based on customer value.

## Business Impact

- **Improved First Contact Resolution (FCR)**: Typical improvements of 25-40%.
- **Lower Operational Costs**: AI handles routine requests at a fraction of human cost.
- **Reduced Handle Time**: Agents spend less time gathering context that the system already has.
- **Higher Customer Satisfaction**: Seamless experiences drive loyalty and reduce churn.

## Common Use Cases

### VIP Fast Track
Detect high-value status and bypass standard queues, routing immediately to a dedicated account manager.

### Proactive Issue Resolution
Detect a service outage and send proactive notifications via SMS or WhatsApp, reducing inbound flood by up to 75%.

### Eliminating "Start Over"
Connect email, web, and phone interactions so the agent can say, "I see you emailed about X. Let me help resolve this."
