# Real-time Contact Center Analytics

ExpertFlow CX delivers a real-time analytics layer that goes beyond live dashboards — combining operational monitoring with AI-powered conversation intelligence to enable proactive decisions during active customer interactions.

---

## Core Analytical Capabilities

### Live Operational Monitoring

- **Queue and Resource Analytics**: Real-time tracking of queue depth, wait times, SLA compliance, and agent status across all channels. Supervisors see a live view of how queues are performing and whether resources are appropriately allocated.
- **Ongoing Conversation Detail**: Real-time metadata and context for every active customer conversation — with both agents and bots — giving supervisors full situational awareness without interrupting interactions.

### Immediate Customer Feedback Integration

Post-interaction CSAT scores and survey responses from CX Surveys are immediately correlated with agent performance data, enabling supervisors to see the quality impact of interactions as soon as they close.

---

## AI-Powered Live Conversation Intelligence

ExpertFlow leverages AI to generate conversation insights **during** the interaction — not just after it ends. This transforms quality management from a post-call review process into live, actionable intelligence.

| AI Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Real-time Sentiment Detection** | Tracks the customer's emotional tone as it shifts during the interaction, flagging negative sentiment escalations instantly. |
| **Live AI-Generated Summaries** | A running synopsis of key discussion points from both bot and agent dialogues, giving supervisors instant context. |
| **Automated Alerting** | Flags configured triggers — profanity, compliance risks, dead-ends in a bot dialog — in real time based on predefined rules. |

---

## Proactive Supervisor Intervention

The system is designed to eliminate the delay between a problem occurring and a supervisor being able to act. Rather than waiting for an agent to escalate, the platform delivers proactive alerts with full context.

### KPI Alerts

A rule-based system generates real-time notifications when KPI thresholds are breached — for example, queue wait time exceeding SLA, agent not-ready duration crossing a threshold, or customer sentiment dropping critically.

### Intervene in Live Conversations

When an at-risk conversation is detected, supervisors can:

- **Barge in with full context**: Join a live call with a complete understanding of the customer issue, including the AI-generated summary of the conversation so far.
- **Coach via Whisper**: Send real-time guidance to the agent without the customer being aware, helping steer the conversation to a positive outcome.

This eliminates the traditional escalation loop — the supervisor acts before the agent needs to formally request help.

---

## Seamless Bot-to-Agent Escalation

When AI detects that a bot is failing to meet a customer's needs (confusion, repeated misunderstanding, or explicit escalation request), the system triggers a warm transfer to a human agent. The agent receives:

- The AI-generated conversation summary
- Full interaction context from the bot session
- The customer's current sentiment state

This ensures a seamless handoff with no need for the customer to repeat themselves.
