# Auditing and Scoring Conversations

As an Evaluator (Eva), your primary mission is to provide objective feedback on customer interactions. This guide covers how to manage your assigned audit queue and complete evaluations accurately.

## 1. Accessing Your Audit Queue
All your work lives in the **Reviews** tab. This is your personal "To-Do" list.

### Managing the List:
- **Status Tracking:** Use the filter to see **Pending** reviews (work to be done) vs. **Completed** reviews (past assessments).
- **Deadlines:** Always sort by **Due Date** to ensure high-priority audits are completed on time.
- **Context:** The list shows which **Questionnaire** (Rubric) is assigned to each conversation, giving you a heads-up on the criteria before you start.

## 2. Performing the Audit
Click the **Start Review** icon on any pending item to enter the unified audit workspace.

### The Unified View:
The system places the conversation data and the evaluation form side-by-side to minimize window switching.
- **Transcripts:** Read the full text of digital interactions (WhatsApp, Webchat).
- **Media Playback:** Use the integrated player to listen to voice recordings or view screen captures.
- **Metadata:** Check the sidebar for agent details, queue info, and interaction timestamps.

## 3. Scoring the Interaction
Fill out the **Evaluation Form** as you review the interaction.

### Best Practices for Eva:
1.  **Objective Scoring:** Base your score strictly on the rubric guidelines provided by the Quality Manager (Quentin).
2.  **Adding Notes:** If you deduct points, add a specific note explaining the reason. These notes are vital for **Agent (Amy)** coaching.
3.  **Submission:** Click **Submit** once all required fields are filled. The system will automatically calculate the final score and move the item to your "Completed" list.

## 4. Notifications & Reminders
Check the **Notifications bell** in the top right for:
- **New Assignments:** Alerts when Quentin assigns a new batch of conversations to you.
- **Deadline Reminders:** Automated alerts for reviews that are approaching their due date.
